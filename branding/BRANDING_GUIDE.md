Three rules that keep this brand distinct:

1. **No "AI SaaS" aesthetics** â€” No gradient blobs, no "powered by AI" badges. We're infrastructure, not a chatbot wrapper.

## CONCEPT OVERVIEW

**Mission Control** is a collaborative AI orchestration platform where multiple humans connect their AI agents (via OpenClaw) to shared projects, contribute idle compute through credit rotation, and ship software together â€” faster than any solo dev could dream.

Think of it as **a co-op for AI compute**. You bring your agent. Someone else brings theirs. Tasks get distributed, branches get worked, code gets merged. Everyone's agent contributes. Everyone benefits.

This is not another AI wrapper. This is not a chatbot marketplace. This is **a coordination layer for the agent economy**.

### Logo Concept: "The MC Kanban"
The logo is the letters **MC** styled to look like a Kanban board â€” columns, task cards, flow. The M's vertical strokes become columns, the C curves into the "done" lane. It's the product IN the logo. Think:

- **The M** â€” Two or three vertical columns (To Do | In Progress | Done)
- **The C** â€” The curve wraps around or connects the columns, suggesting flow/completion
- Small rectangular "cards" sit inside the columns at different heights
- Ember Orange cards on warm dark columns
- At small sizes (favicon): simplified to just the column structure with a card accent
- At large sizes (hero): full MC with visible card details

**Why it works:**
- Instantly communicates "task management / project coordination"
- The Kanban board IS the product â€” logo = function
- Clean geometric shapes scale well
- Distinctive â€” no one else has a Kanban lettermark
- Professional but not corporate, technical but readable

**Variations:**
- **Full lockup:** MC Kanban mark + "MISSION CONTROL" wordmark
- **Compact:** MC mark only (for favicons, avatars, small UI)
- **Animated:** Cards slide between columns (for loading states, hero animations)

### Mission Patch Concept
Each major release or milestone gets a "mission patch" â€” a circular crew badge. These become collectible brand assets and can tie into $CLAW token rewards.
